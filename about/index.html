About

Hi, I’m Brett!

I am an embedded software developer from Edmonton AB, Canada. In 2016 I graduated from the University of Alberta, located here in Edmonton, with a BSc. in Electrical Engineering. I am interested in developing a broader and deeper understanding of the entire hardware and software stack of our computing platforms, from semiconductor physics, digital circuit design, RTL and VLSI, to assembly programming, low level systems programming, algorithms, and beyond.
